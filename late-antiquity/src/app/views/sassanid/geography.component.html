One of the oldest locations of civilization, Persia lies at a geographical center of three continents.
Stretching from India and Tibet in the east, to Roman Syria and Egypt in the west, the empire was a multiculture melting pot of peoples, ideas, trade, and more.
The borders frequently grew and shrank over time, as the empire was subjuct to frequent invasions from all sides.
